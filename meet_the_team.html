Meet The Founder!
Pst. Prosper Paul Eshun



Pst. Prosper Paul Eshun is the CEO, Founder and Programme Manager of Gloryland Realty Foundation (GRF). He is also a dedicated Pastor in Winners’ Chapel International (Living Faith Church Worldwide) under the spiritual covering of Bishop David O. Oyedepo.

A committed member of the Living Faith Church Worldwide since September 1998, Pastor Eshun was officially ordained on April 20, 2011. With over twenty-eight (28) years of unwavering service in the Commission, he has faithfully served as a Pastor for the past sixteen (15) years and continues to do so with passion and excellence.
He has planted numerous churches and remains actively involved in the mission. He currently serves as the Resident Pastor and Pastor-in-Charge of one of the branches under the Kasoa District Church in the Central Region of Ghana.

Pastor Eshun is happily married to his beloved wife, “bone of my bone”- he usually calls her, and they are blessed with three children.
