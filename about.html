The Story Behind GRF!

The Gloryland Realty Foundation was registered in November, 2022 and fully certified by the Department of Social Welfare Certificate of Recognition as a non-profit organization on 29th May, 2023.

Founded by the CEO, Pst. Prosper Paul Eshun of Winner’s Chapel International and Living Faith Church World Wide, with the goal to change the lives of both women and the youth. He hoped to train and empower vulnerable youth and women in deprived communities to obtain employable skills or better skill, self-employable skills to fend for themselves and their families.

Inspired by the life journey of his beloved diseased sister (may her soul rest in perfect peace), he built GRF in hopes that other women may benefit the same way his sister did. In her young days, she was trained by an elderly woman in her church whom after retiring from baking, decided to train women and the youth of her church on how to make delightful pastries. That is where the beloved sister of our CEO took up the advantage she had and made good use of it. She started a business and ended up hiring a number of workers who helped her run the bakery. As she did so, she never forgot about the society and stretched out her arms to help vulnerable families.

Today, GRF has proved its worth, as quite a number of youth, women and families have been reached out to, and impacted positively.

Here at GRF, we hope to reduce unemployment in the youth and women in our country, and empowering the vulnerable in the society.

Yet we can not do this on our own! That is why we are calling for all the support we can get. Become a partner, or support us however way you can.
We will be glad to receive you.
